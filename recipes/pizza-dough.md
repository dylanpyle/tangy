# Pizza Dough

## Ingredients

- 1 cup warm water
- 1 packet active dry yeast
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1.5 teaspoons salt
- 2.5 cups flour
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Mix water, yeast, and sugar in a medium-sized bowl.
2. Mix in salt and flour. When flour is fully mixed, knead by hand for 5 minutes.
3. Brush the dough with a small amount of olive oil.
4. Cover the bowl, and let the dough rise until doubled in size â€” about 1 hour.
